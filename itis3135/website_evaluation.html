Website Evaluation

Worst Website

The beta version of Reddit was launched for users to test new features, functions, and design changes but it currently looks to be in an abandoned state. This website does not follow CRAP principles.

  • C - Contrast

    • Contrast could be improved by making certain elements more visually distinguishable.

  • R - Repetition

    • The homepage of the website shows recommended reddit posts all over the webpage without much variation in terms of content.

  • A - Alignment

    • The alignemnt for the reddit posts displayed on the homepage is not properly akigned making it hard for the user to focus on a single post.

  • P - Proximity

    • All the posts are closely together which may lead to confusion.

Best Website

Apple is a technology company, famous for its innovative consumer electronics, software, tech, and services, including iPhone, iPad, and macOS. This website does follow CRAP principles.

  • C - Contrast

    • Apple uses sharp contrasts in high-quality images to emphasize product details, enhancing visual appeal and engagement.

  • R - Repetition

    • Consistent branding and navigation elements, ensuring a seamless user experience.

  • A - Alignment

    • Grid layout and precise content alignment create a clean, organized, and visually appealing website design.

  • P - Proximity

    • Grouping related content enhances user decision-making, while effective white space usage promotes order and hierarchy.
